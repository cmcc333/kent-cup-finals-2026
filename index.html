Welcome to Sparrows Den

Beccehamian RFC is incredibly proud to be hosting this year's Kent Cup Girls U16s and U18s Finals Day. Kent has some of the most incredible girls' rugby in the UK and we will be seeing some truly fantastic matches today. Please enjoy the day and support all teams in the full spirit of rugby.

Road Closure — Corkscrew Hill

Bromley Council has advised that major roadworks will close Corkscrew Hill at its junction with Addington Road on the day of the final. Signed diversions will be in place. Please allow extra travel time and follow diversion signage carefully.

This section will be updated closer to the event as we receive more detail from Bromley Council.

Detailed parking maps and full diversion route plans will be published on this page as soon as we learn more about the impact of the roadworks which have not yet started. Bookmark this page and check back closer to 26 April.

P1

Limited On-Street Parking

P2

Beccehamian RFC Car Park — Closed

The club car park is reserved throughout the day for match officials, first aid, and event operations. No public parking.

No parking on grass verges. Wardens will be operating. Please respect cones and driveways.

Please car share wherever possible. Parking is very limited and the road closure will reduce access. The more people we can get in fewer cars, the better.

Bringing a minibus? Please contact the club in advance so we can allocate appropriate space. Contact Chris McCandless before the day.

Facilities

Food & Drink

Our bar — with its brand new terrace overlooking the pitches — will be open throughout the day. A great range of food will be served from either the bar or a pitch side catering tent.

💧

Tap Water

Tap water is available on an external tap on the clubhouse under the scoreboard.

🚻

Toilets

Toilets are available in the clubhouse. A number of portaloos are also located on site.

🏉

Changing Rooms

Our brand new changing rooms are available for all players. No team allocations will be made. Please do not leave possessions behind.

⛺

Gazebos & Shelters

Gazebos are welcome but please monitor wind conditions — Sparrows Den can be exposed. All structures must be pegged and supervised.

🐶

Dogs

Dogs are welcome but must be kept on leads at all times. Please clean up after your pet.

Match Officials

Referees for today's finals have been kindly provided by two of England's most respected referee societies. We are very grateful for their support.

L

London Society of Rugby Football Union Referees

Founded over 135 years ago, the LSRFUR fields more than 500 qualified referees who officiate over 10,000 games each season across London and beyond.
